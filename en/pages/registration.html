Consular registration

The Consulate of Armenia in Nepal is pleased to announce the opening of registration for Armenian citizens residing in Nepal. This initiative aims to strengthen the connection between the Armenian community and the Consulate, ensuring that all citizens receive important updates and support.

Armenian citizens leaving for a foreign country for residence of more than 6 months or residing in a foreign country for more than 6 months are required to notify in writing the appropriate embassy or consular office of the Republic of Armenia.

To register, please fill out the registration form available on our website and submit it to the Consulate. The information provided will be kept confidential and used solely for consular purposes.

We encourage all Armenian citizens in Nepal to take advantage of this opportunity to stay connected with the Consulate and receive important updates and support.

For any questions or assistance with the registration process, please contact the Consulate directly.

Thank you for your cooperation, and we look forward to serving you.

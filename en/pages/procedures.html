VISA for citizens of Nepal

VISA TO ENTER ARMENIA IS MANDATORY; IT IS NECESSARY TO OBTAIN IN ADVANCE THROUGH THE E-VISA SYSTEM OR BY APPLYING TO THE DIPLOMATIC MISSIONS OF THE REPUBLIC OF ARMENIA IN FOREIGN COUNTRIES (THOSE CITIZENS WHO TRAVEL TO ARMENIA FROM THIRD COUNTRIES).

WE REPEAT ONCE AGAIN; IT IS NOT POSSIBLE TO GET ARMENIAN VISA UPON ARRIVAL AT THE BORDER CHECKPOINTS OF THE REPUBLIC OF ARMENIA.

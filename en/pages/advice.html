Advice for travellers

To the attention of the citizens of Armenia visiting Bulgaria 01.03.2020

The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Armenia recommends that citizens of Armenia traveling to Bulgaria be vigilant and take the necessary precautions to ensure their safety. In particular, it is recommended to avoid large gatherings of people, to refrain from visiting places where there is a high risk of infection, to follow the instructions of the local authorities and to keep in touch with the Armenian Embassy in Sofia.

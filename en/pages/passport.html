RENEWAL OR OBTAINING OF PASSPORT FOR THE FIRST TIME FOR A MINOR UNDER THE AGE OF 16

A passport of a citizen of the Republic of Armenia is issued to citizens under the age of 16 upon request, based on the application submitted by one parent or a legal representative in person. Passports are issued for a 3-year validity period to minors under the age of 6, for a 5-year validity period to minors from 6 to 16, and until the passport holder turns 19 to male citizens under the age of 16.

List of documents required for obtaining a passport:

  1. Application form
  2. Birth certificate of the child
  3. Passport of one of the parents or legal representative
  4. Two 3x4 cm photos
  5. Receipt for payment of state duty

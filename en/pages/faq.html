FAQ


  1. How do citizens of Nepal, holding an ordinary passport, obtain an entry visa to the Republic of Armenia?

    Citizens of Nepal obtain Armenian entry visa:

    • Through e-Visa Issuance System.
    • By applying to any Embassy of Armenia abroad with the documents mentioned below:
      1. Visa application form with one passport-size colore photo
      2. Passport and its photocopy
      3. The printout of the online payment of the visa fee
